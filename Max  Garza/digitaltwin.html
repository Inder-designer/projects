What are Digital Twin

Digital twins are virtual replicas of physical objects or systems. They are created using advanced software and technologies, such as sensors, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. These digital twins can be used to monitor and analyze real-world systems, predict their behavior, and optimize their performance.

Why Digital twin

Digital twins have become increasingly popular in recent years due to their ability to improve operational efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance productivity. They are used in various industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, construction, and transportation, to name a few.
