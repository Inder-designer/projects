What do we do

ESG a block chain based marketplace, where buyers & sellers meet to carry out operations involving digital goods & assets with crypto currency transactions. Our stablecoins are fully collateralized, held in third-party escrow, and subject to live, independent on-chain attestations

Why ESG

  • Enable fast transfer and transaction times.
  • Power business payments between merchants and customers.
  • Eliminate payment delays.
  • Encourage Peer to Peer (P2P) transactions.

Usecase

Works as safe “parking space” for crypto volatility. Serves as a bridge between fiat currencies and crypto-assets.

To buy products and services on partner networks

To pay for transaction fees on ESG

To buy assets and earn rewards within the ESG system

Eventually, for trading & swapping once accepted on larger crypto networks.
